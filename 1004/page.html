OUR STORY

Ahmad Tea is a UK-based family business, drawing on four generations of tea blending and tasting experience. Founded on a passion for the finest tea, we are dedicated to the unique place that tea drinking commands at the heart of daily life in Britain and beyond. When the company was established in England, we felt that people desired an exceptional infusion which could be enjoyed every day. The selection of our first plantations and partners, our initial blending and tasting, and even our early packaging – all were driven by a commitment to meet this need. To this end, we were also one of the first companies to present tea in ways that emphasised its worth, offering it in beautiful gift packs, exquisite tins and charming caddies. Early in the life of Ahmad Tea, our Managing Director promised, “I will not sell anything that I would not drink at home.” This refusal to accept anything but the finest teas – primarily in terms of their flavour, aroma, strength and consistency continues to guide our tasters and inspire our entire company.

Tea Pick & Mix

Black Teas

Black teas make the perfect cup of tea. We offer a range of classic blends in teabags and loose leaf to help get you through the day. Kickstart the morning with an energising English Breakfast and then wind down in the evening with a calming caffeine-free blend. There’s a black tea for any occasion – from afternoon tea to a comforting everyday brew. Simply add milk or lemon and enjoy your classic cuppa.

Green Teas

Green tea was amongst the first tea to be exported by China and has been grown and processed there for over 5,000 years. Unlike black teas, the leaves aren’t allowed to oxidise and so stay green. This gives green tea its light, delicate flavour and pale green/yellow colour. A top source of antioxidants, green tea makes for a relaxing and fragrant drink to be enjoyed at any time of day.

Fruit & Herbal Teas

Fruit and herbal teas offer a different kind of hot drink. With all the taste and warming comfort of tea, they don’t feature any leaves from the tea plant. Instead, they’re infused with natural herbs and fruits to create vibrant flavours, vivid colours and enticing aromas. From soothing camomile to revitalising ginger, our herbal teas offer an infusion for every moment. And being caffeine free, sugar free and made with 100% natural ingredients, they’re a feel-good, guilt-free treat.

Fruit Black Teas

Our fruit black teas combine our quality black tea with the zesty sweetness of fruits. Each blend is made with real fruit pieces to create distinctive, authentic flavours. Be transported to the tropics with exotic citrus fruits or taste the British summertime with succulent berries. Our fruit-flavoured black teas can be enjoyed hot or cold – great for warming up in the winter or cooling down in the summer. Explore the range and discover this refreshing and tangy twist on traditional tea.

Cold Brew Iced Teas

Our Cold Brew Iced Teas are a quick and convenient way to enjoy freshly brewed iced tea. The teabags brew in cold water in just a few minutes – no need for boiling water. Boasting less caffeine than hot tea and with no added sugar or sweeteners, they’re a great alternative to soft drinks. Our range includes black and green tea blends - offering the perfect summer cold drink for the whole family.
